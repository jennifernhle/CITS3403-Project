What is Popcorn Journal?

Popcorn Journal is the ultimate companion for film enthusiasts. Log your latest movie theatre adventures in seconds. Manage your watchlist, rate your favourites, and keep a digital journal of your personal watchlog.

Track your personal cinematic journey

Log all the movies you've watched, are currently binging, and want to see.

Rate and review

Our review journal lets you capture exactly how a film made you feel. Share your takes with the community, or keep them as a private digital diary.

Curate and organise your films with lists

Create lists to organise and sort your collection of films exactly how you want. Whether that be "Films Directed by Women" to "Cozy Rainy Day Movies", you can search for public lists, share your list with the community, or keep it as a personal, private log.
