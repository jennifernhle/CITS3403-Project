About Popcorn Journal

Popcorn Journal is for film enthusiasts who find it hard to keep track of all the movies they have watched and want to watch. This journal is an easy-to-use platform to log movie history, manage watchlists, and share ratings and reviews with others.

Track

Keep a digital diary of every movie you've ever seen.

Rate

Share your thoughts and scores with a community of fellow enthusiasts.

Discover

Explore trending titles and curated lists to find your new favourite film.
